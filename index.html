KCE Confluence 2024 is a premier national-level fest organized by the Computer Science and Engineering Department, aiming to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application while fostering innovation, creativity, and collaboration among students. The event features a diverse range of technical competitions, such as coding contests, hackathons, and workshops, alongside engaging non-technical activities like debate competitions, quizzes, and cultural performances. Participants will have the opportunity to network with peers and industry professionals, building valuable connections for future endeavors. Join us for an enriching experience filled with learning, competition, and camaraderie at KCE Confluence 2024!
